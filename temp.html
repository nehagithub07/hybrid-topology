Network Topology: Hybrid (Hub Only)


To perform the simulation, follow the below given steps:
1. Click on either PC or laptop to place devices for the first star topology.
2. Click on hub to add the central device.
3. Connect the devices to create the first star topology.
NOTE: Click a device port and drag to another device port to connect.
4. Click on the check button to verify the first star topology.
5. Add devices for the second star topology.
6. Add another hub.
7. Connect the devices to create the second star topology.
8. Verify the second star topology.
9. Connect the two hubs to create the hybrid topology.
10. Click check to verify the complete hybrid topology.
