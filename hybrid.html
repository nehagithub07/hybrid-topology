Network Topology: Hybrid


To perform the simulation (choose one from each row):
End devices: PC or Laptop
Central devices: Hub or Switch

Steps:
1. Click on PC or Laptop once to place 5 end devices (1..5).
2. Click on Hub or Switch once to place the two central devices (1..2).
3. Connect to match the diagram (1/2/3 → device-1, device-1 ↔ device-2, 4/5 → device-2).
NOTE: Click a device port and drag to another device port to connect.
4. Click Check to validate the hybrid topology.
